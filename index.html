JFK Exhibition

12pm - 7pm Tuesday March 26 to Friday and 10am - 4pm Saturday March 30

10am - 12:30pm & 1:00pm to 4:00pm

“JFK: 50 Years Later” exhibition week is here! We are so thankful to our generous sponsors for making the events of this week possible. All venues are open 12pm to 7pm Tuesday through Friday, and 10am to 4pm on Saturday. The exhibition, presented by Rogers Photo Archives and Argenta Images, includes photos that have never or rarely been seen. The photos focus on various aspects of the President’s life, including family, early years in politics, critical issues and images of the times, and his Presidency. The exclusive photos will be on display in the Argenta Arts District galleries and exhibition space in downtown North Little Rock, home of Rogers Photo Archive and Argenta Images. Eight North Little Rock galleries and restaurants will serve as hosts of the approximately 120 images.
